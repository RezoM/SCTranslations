What is ISMN-13
A 13-digit identifier for editions of notated music, encoded using the EAN-13 barcode format with a 979-0 prefix.
Purpose of ISMN-13
To uniquely identify printed and digital music publications under the ISO and GS1/EAN systems.
Abbreviation Meaning
“International Standard Music Number” (13-digit format since 2008).
Where It's Used
  • Printed sheet music
  • Digital sheet music
  • Music publishers
  • Libraries and archives
